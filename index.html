ABOUT ME

Ever since I built my first gaming computer with my father when I was 10, I’ve been excited by all-things software and tech. As I grew up, I further explored my interest through CS courses in high school and college, ultimately attaining a Computer Science bachelor’s degree from Emory University. Alongside CS, I double majored in Finance to further explore my interest in the financial markets.

After university, I spent a few years in technology investment banking and venture capital, experiences that provided exposure to both software and finance. In 2022, I completed the 16-week SWE bootcamp App Academy and made the decision to transition into software engineering to build the software I was previously investing in. Since then, I've joined the micro-investing app as a frontend developer on the Web team.

My experiences in finance working in fast-paced environments coupled with my technical degrees have well-prepared me, and I look forward to working as a full-stack software engineer, bringing along a diversified skillset across programming, investing, and financial analysis.
